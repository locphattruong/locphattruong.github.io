Badminton Self-Learning

I could have chosen to learn badminton from a tutor, but I genuinely enjoy figuring things out by myself. Three years ago, I was a complete beginner in the sport, just like anyone starting something new. I watched countless tutorials and self-taught the fundamentals. I focused on key aspects like basic swings, body mechanics to efficiently create a powerful-yet-natural movement using my wrist, arm, shoulder, and hips. I took a bottom-up approach, mastering one element at a time, and prioritizing consistency over speed. The most crucial part of my self-learning journey was feedback. I started recording my sessions, analyzing my movements, and comparing them with pro players to ensure I was on track.
